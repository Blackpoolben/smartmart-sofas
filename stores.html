Important — please read

We will never ask you to send money online.

There are fake accounts and fake listings using our name, our photos and our stock. They are not us.

Smart Mart takes payment in the showroom only. Never by bank transfer. Never by a link in a message. Never over the phone, even if the person seems to know what you were looking at. If anyone claiming to be Smart Mart asks you to send money, stop, and ring the store directly on the number above to check with us first.

You will not offend anyone by checking. We would much rather you rang us ten times than lost a penny to someone pretending to be us.

How it works

Reserving online, buying in person.

1. Reserve what you like

Browse the range and add anything you fancy to your list. It costs nothing, we do not ask for a card, and we do not ask for a deposit.

2. Come and sit on it

Bring your list to Blackpool or Newcastle. We will hold the items for seven days. Try them properly — a sofa you have not sat on is a guess.

3. Pay at the counter

Only when you are sure. We will sort delivery, check your access measurements, and give you a receipt with our name on it.
