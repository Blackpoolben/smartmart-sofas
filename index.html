Important — please read

We will never ask you to send money online.

There are fake accounts using our name and our photos. They are not us. Smart Mart takes payment in the showroom only — never by bank transfer, never by a link in a message, never over the phone. If anyone claiming to be us asks you to send money, stop, and ring the store on the number on our showrooms page to check. You will not offend us by checking. We would far rather you did.
